Exam Rules

Read these rules carefully before you begin. Warnings are recorded during the exam, and the exam will end automatically if the warning limit is reached.

  • Your camera must stay on before and during the exam.
  • Leaving fullscreen or kiosk mode counts as a warning.
  • Switching away from the exam window or hiding it counts as a warning.
  • Blocked apps such as browsers, chat apps, or meeting apps may be closed automatically.
  • Printing during the exam counts as a warning.
  • Leaving the exam before submission may submit your attempt immediately.
  • Each warning increases your warning count and is saved in the proctoring log.
  • When the warning limit is reached, the exam is submitted automatically and cannot be resumed.
