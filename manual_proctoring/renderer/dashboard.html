Exam Rules And Violations

Read these rules carefully. Every violation will be recorded, each warning will trigger a loud beep, and your exam will be permanently terminated after the warning limit is reached.

  • A working camera is mandatory before the exam can start.
  • Leaving fullscreen or kiosk mode is a warning.
  • Switching away from the exam window or hiding the page is a warning.
  • Opening blocked apps like browsers, chat apps, or meeting apps is a warning and those apps will be closed automatically.
  • Trying to print during the exam is a warning.
  • Leaving the exam before submission may submit the exam immediately.
  • Every warning increases your warning count and is written to the proctoring log.
  • At the warning limit, the exam is auto-submitted and cannot be resumed.
